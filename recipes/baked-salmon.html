Baked Salmon

Description

A fool proof oven-baked salmon dish that is easy to make and big on flavor. If you don't like dill, you could try other fresh herbs, or even a mixture of dill, parsley, and spring onion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped fresh dill
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely minced or more to taste
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (2.5 pound) boneless salmon fillet
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
